HELLO!

if the game makes "beep", "boop", or any weird noise... that was me.

I'm a Technical Sound Designer and Gameplay Audio Programmer with over 10 years of professional experience creating interactive audio for games. I've contributed to titles such as Nekome, Orcs Must Die! By the Blade, and Killer Klowns From Outer Space, building gameplay audio systems and implementing audio using technologies like Unreal Engine, FMOD, Wwise, Unity, and C++.

Beyond game development, I'm passionate about sharing knowledge with the game audio community. My talks have been featured at events including GDC, Game Audio Conference Oslo, and Game Sound Summit Puerto Rico. One of the highlights of my career was being selected as a member of The Game Awards Future Class 2023.
