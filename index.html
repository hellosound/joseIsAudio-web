if the game makes "beep", "boop", or any weird noise... that was me.

I'm a sound designer and game audio programmer with over 10 years of professional experience. I have worked on games like Nekome, Orcs Must Die!, By The Blade and Killer Klowns From Outer Space, using various technologies.

Finally, I love public speaking; my talks have been featured at events like GDC, Game Audio Conference Oslo and Game Sound Summit Puerto Rico. One of the biggest highlights of my career was being selected as a member of The Game Awards Future Class 2023.
